About BookNGo

BookNGo is a tour operator in India which organizes and conducts individual tours and excursions in India since 2023.

We help to travel safely and comfortably, away from noisy and large groups of tourists, because only in this way you can feel the beauty of the world and stay alone with nature.

The main mode of transport in our company is full-drive off-road vehicles. Jeeps will take our customers to the most remote places in India. Time saving, comfort and clear pictures while driving are only a part of what this mode of transport can boast. When travelling long distances, rest on the road is very important.

  • Are you planning to visit India?
  • Have you already bought tickets to Kashmir or Goa and are looking for a tour?
  • Are you already in India or Delhi and want to go on a trip the next day?
  • A tour bought with another company cancelled and you want to start on a trip as soon as possible?

In all these cases, please contact us and we will definitely help you. Our consultants answer within 2 hours and are available 24 hours 7 days a week.

The cost to travel varies based on factors like destination, duration, travel style, season, and activities. On average, budget travelers in India might spend ₹1,500 to ₹5,000 per day, while mid-range and luxury travelers could spend ₹5,000 to ₹15,000 or more. Creating a rough itinerary can help you estimate your total expenses more accurately.

When planning to travel, know these key points:
  • 1.Research Destination: Learn about local culture and attractions.
  • 2.Budget: Estimate costs for flights, accommodation, food, and activities.
  • 3.Travel Documents: Ensure you have visas and travel insurance.
  • 4.Health and Safety: Check vaccinations and safety tips.
  • 5.Packing: Bring appropriate clothing for the climate.
  • 6.Emergency Contacts: Keep local emergency numbers handy.
